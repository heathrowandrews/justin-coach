Fit Check

Is This Right For You?

You're a good fit if you:

  • Want to feel capable and confident in your body
  • Care about how you move, not just how you look
  • Value long-term progress over quick fixes
  • Are willing to slow down to move forward

Probably not the right fit if you:

  • Want a rapid transformation or challenge
  • Prefer maximal intensity all the time
  • Want a generic program with no adjustment
  • Aren't open to modifying when life requires it
