Background

I've been in the arena since I was five. Gymnastics, baseball, football, and a D1 track scholarship running hurdles at UTSA.

Four sports. Seventeen years of competition. That taught me something no certification can: performance is never just physical. How you think, recover, and make decisions under pressure determines everything.

I studied the science too. Kinesiology degree. Exercise science concentration. Minors in sociology and wellness. Then I got certified through ACSM and earned a mindset certification through NCI. The academic foundation backs up what the years of competing already proved.

Today I also guide kayak tours in Crystal River, Florida, spending my days on the water with manatees and springs. That work keeps me rooted in the same things I coach: presence, patience, and showing up fully.

I'm not handing you a workout plan. I'm helping you close the gap between who you are and who you know you can be.
